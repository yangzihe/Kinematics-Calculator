How it works

For kinematics equations, there are five variables:

acceleration, displacement, intial velocity, current velocity, time

In any particular question, one of these variables will not need to be considered at all. For this variable, enter x in its corresponding field. For the variable you are trying to solve for, enter ? in its corresponding field. The rest of the values should be given in problem; enter these accordingly.
